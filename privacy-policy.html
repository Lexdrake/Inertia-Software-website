Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

Inertia Software LLC ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our products and services, including our flagship application, Inertia - Planner.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address for account creation and communication
  • Username and display name for personalization
  • Profile picture (optional) for account customization
  • Authentication credentials (securely encrypted)

1.2 User-Generated Content

  • Goals, projects, tasks, and to-do items
  • Habits, routines, and recurring schedules
  • Class schedules and academic information
  • Work expectations and professional data
  • Personal notes and custom categories

1.3 Usage Data

  • App interaction patterns and feature usage statistics
  • Scheduling preferences and mode preferences
  • Time spent in different app sections
  • Feature adoption and workflow patterns

1.4 Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version
  • App version and build number
  • Unique device identifiers for sync purposes
  • Time zone and locale settings

1.5 Social Features (Optional)

  • Friend connections and social network within the app
  • Shared posts, comments, and collaborative content
  • Group memberships and team data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide and maintain the Inertia - Planner service and enable core functionality
  • Data Synchronization: To synchronize your data across multiple devices seamlessly
  • Notifications: To send important notifications about your scheduled items (with your permission)
  • Improvements: To improve app performance, user experience, and develop new features
  • Customer Support: To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Security: To ensure security, prevent fraud, and protect against unauthorized access
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and optimize the application (using anonymized data)

3. Data Storage & Protection

3.1 Security Measures

  • All data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS encryption
  • Personal data is stored on secure Supabase servers with encryption at rest
  • Authentication handled through secure, industry-standard protocols (OAuth 2.0, JWT)
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Automatic backups to prevent data loss
  • Strict access controls limiting employee data access on a need-to-know basis

3.2 Data Centers

Your data is stored in secure data centers operated by our infrastructure partners, primarily Supabase and Amazon Web Services (AWS), which maintain industry-leading security certifications including SOC 2, ISO 27001, and PCI DSS compliance.

4. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

4.1 Access

Request a copy of all personal data we have about you in a structured, machine-readable format.

4.2 Correction

Update or correct any inaccurate information through your account settings or by contacting support.

4.3 Deletion

Request deletion of your account and all associated data. We will comply within 30 days unless legally required to retain certain information.

4.4 Portability

Export your data in JSON or CSV format for use in other applications or for your records.

4.5 Opt-out

Disable optional features like social sharing, analytics tracking, or marketing communications.

4.6 Notification Preferences

Control which notifications you receive and how you receive them through your app settings.

5. Data Sharing

5.1 Our Commitment

We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your privacy is fundamental to our business model.

5.2 Limited Sharing

We may share your data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With essential service providers who help us operate our services (e.g., Supabase for data storage, Sentry for error tracking, Stripe for payments)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental request
  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Inertia Software LLC, our users, or the public
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)
  • Aggregated Data: Anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify individuals may be used for analytics and research

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained as long as your account remains active and for the duration needed to provide services
  • Deleted Accounts: Personal data removed within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Legal Compliance: Some anonymized data may be retained for legal compliance or legitimate business purposes
  • Backup Data: Backup data retained for 90 days for recovery purposes before permanent deletion
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated analytics data retained indefinitely in anonymized form

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that your data remains protected to the standards described in this policy.

9. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold. California residents may also request deletion of their personal information and opt-out of the sale of their personal information (though we do not sell personal information).

10. Updates to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements
  • Users will be notified via email of significant privacy policy changes at least 30 days before they take effect
  • Continued use of our services after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
  • Previous versions of this policy are available upon request
