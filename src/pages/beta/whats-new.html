📝 About Beta Versions

Each major beta (v1.0, v2.0) may have multiple sub-versions (v1.1, v1.2, v2.1, v2.2, etc.) focused on:

  • 🐛 Bug fixes and stability improvements
  • 🎨 UI/UX refinements based on your feedback
  • ⚡ Performance optimizations
  • 🔧 Minor feature adjustments

The exact number of sub-versions depends on what we discover during testing - your feedback drives these improvements!
