🎯 App Philosophy: Overcoming Inertia

Inertia Planner is built on a simple physics principle: objects at rest stay at rest, objects in motion stay in motion. The app helps you get organized, create actionable plans, and know exactly what you can be doing at any moment to stay in motion toward your goals.

The Three Life Modes

🎓

Student Mode

Manage classes, assignments, projects, and academic habits

💼

Work Mode

Track professional expectations, projects, and routines

🌟

Life Mode

Set personal goals and manage all aspects of life

Pro Tip: Each mode works independently! You can disable modes you don't need, or use all three for complete life management.

🎓 Student Mode

Perfect for managing your academic life with classes, assignments, and study habits.

📚 Classes

Create your classes, then add assignments and test prep items within each class.

Example: Create "Chemistry 101" → Add "Lab Report Due Friday" and "Midterm Study Sessions"

📋 Class Projects

Big projects? Break them down into milestones and tasks assigned to specific classes.

Example: "Senior Thesis" → Milestones: Research, First Draft, Revisions → Tasks within each milestone

🎯 Academic Habits

Set time-based goals to maintain, increase, or decrease study habits.

Example: "Read for 30 minutes daily" or "Reduce social media during study time"

Student Mode Tabs:

  • School: ✅ Manage classes and school projects
  • Habits: ✅ Track academic habits
  • Home: ✅ Day view of your planner
  • Planner: ✅ Batch scheduling and 3-day view
  • Groups: 🔄 Coming in Beta v2.0 - Collaborate on group projects

💼 Work Mode

Designed for professionals managing expectations, projects, and work routines.

🎯 Expectations

Create deliverable-based or metric-based expectations with check-in schedules. These cascade based on timeframe (annual → quarterly → monthly → weekly → daily).

Example: Annual goal: "Increase sales by 20%" → Quarterly check-ins to track progress

📈 Work Projects

Just like student projects - create milestones and tasks, but focused on professional work.

Example: "Website Redesign" → Milestones: Research, Design, Development, Launch

🔄 Routines

Set recurring items with specific start/end times on certain days. No reporting needed - just scheduled structure.

Example: "Team standup Monday-Friday 9:00-9:15 AM"

Work Mode Tabs:

  • Work: ✅ Manage expectations and work projects
  • Routines: ✅ Set up recurring work activities
  • Home: ✅ Day view of your planner
  • Planner: ✅ Batch scheduling and 3-day view
  • Teams: 🔄 Coming in Beta v2.0 - Collaborate with colleagues

🌟 Life Mode

Example: Goal: "Get healthier" → Project: "Gym routine" → Habit: "Work out 3x per week"

🔄 Smart Goal Creation (Coming in v2.0)

AI-powered goal creation assistance with automatic project and habit generation.

🔄 Events & Vacation Planning (Coming in v2.0)

Manage holidays, important dates, and vacation planning with optional check-ins for memories.

🔄 Social Features (Coming in v2.0)

Connect with friends, share group calendars, and organize social events.

Life Mode Tabs:

  • Goals: ✅ Set and track personal goals
  • Events: 🔄 Coming in Beta v2.0 - Calendar view and vacation planning
  • Home: ✅ Day view of your planner

📅 Using the Central Planner

The magic happens here - all your items from different modes come together in one organized planner.

🗂️ All Items Tab

See everything you've created across all modes. Use batch scheduling to:

  1. Select specific items you want to schedule
  2. Move to the weekly view scheduling screen
  3. Click an item, then click a time slot to schedule it
  4. Valid time slots appear in green

📱 Main Planner Tab

Your 3-day view shows what's coming up. Click any item to see scheduling details or reschedule.

✅ Productivity Tab

Report on your progress:

  • Mark tasks and assignments as complete
  • Report on habits (can set habits as 'omitted' if you prefer not to report)
  • Track your momentum and stay accountable

💡 Pro Tips for Success

🚀 Start Small

Begin with one mode that fits your current situation. You can always add more later!

🎯 Use Batch Scheduling

Instead of scheduling items one by one, select multiple items and schedule them all at once in the weekly view.

📊 Check Your Progress

Regularly use the Productivity tab to mark things complete and see how you're building momentum.

🔄 Build Routines

Use Work Mode routines for any recurring activities, even personal ones like "Morning workout".

🎭 Mix and Match

Don't feel locked into categories - use Student Mode for personal learning projects or Work Mode for freelance work.

📱 Daily Check-ins

Make it a habit to check your 3-day planner view each morning to see what's ahead.
