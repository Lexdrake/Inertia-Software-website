üè∑Ô∏è The Category System

Throughout development, we built a comprehensive categorization system that powers many features:

Work

Professional tasks and career goals

Health

Physical and mental wellness activities

Learning

Education, skill development, and knowledge

Rest

Relaxation, sleep, and downtime

Social

Relationships, family, and community

Personal

Personal development and hobbies

Financial

Money management and financial goals

Other

Miscellaneous activities (default)

How Categories Power the App:

  • Planner Sections: Create time blocks where only certain categories can be scheduled
  • Difficulty Settings: Set caps based on item difficulties (1-5) and durations
  • Smart Scheduling: AI considers categories when creating optimal schedules
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor progress across different life areas
