Current Opening

Content & Community Manager

Remote Marketing

Help us build and engage a community around Inertia - Planner, our upcoming life management app. You'll create content across social platforms and foster meaningful connections with our user base.

What You'll Do

  • Create engaging content for TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, X, and LinkedIn
  • Build and nurture our community of beta testers and early users
  • Develop and maintain our brand voice across all platforms
  • Foster engagement through meaningful interactions and community initiatives
  • Execute cross-platform content strategy aligned with product launches

What We're Looking For

  • Experience with productivity, wellness apps, or similar audiences
  • Deep understanding of Gen Z/millennial productivity challenges
  • Strong content creation skills (copywriting, basic design)
  • Analytics and community management experience
  • Self-motivated and thrives in remote work environments
  • Passion for technology that improves people's daily lives

Compensation & Benefits

  • Limited compensation due to being a startup
  • Fully remote position
  • Flexible working hours
  • Direct collaboration with founders
  • Opportunity to shape a growing product and brand
  • Early equity consideration for exceptional performance

Don't See the Right Fit?

We're always interested in connecting with talented individuals who share our vision. If you think you'd be a great addition to our team, we'd love to hear from you.
